Bio

I was born and raised in the city of Detroit, MI. Ever since I was little I always wanted to be a video game programmer, funny right? lol. Usually when your a kid you dream about being a superhero or a sports pro, or a doctor...something along those lines.

I was kind of in and out of college after I graduated from Crockett Technical High School (went to IADT for about a year for game design that didn't work out and then briefly attended OCC to try and start on a degree for Computer Information Systems which didn't work out). After that I had my friend tell me about a call center job working for Apple fixing peoples issues with iPhones and I jumped at the opportunity since I was raised in an Apple electronics family.

I was there for about a year and some change before I found my next call center job at HP in Pontiac, MI (funny enough, my friend also ended up working there with me as well) and it was also where I found out about IT and the various programs that are used to help businesses operate properly and fix issues. I liked working with those programs and fixing issues that may arise. I had been a part of many IT organizations afterwards such as Stefanini, Caretech, and CDK Global.

What made me make the transfer over was after I left CDK I had my other friend tell me about Grand Circus and the various programs that they have as far as programming and coding in which he told me to goto an intro to coding class and see what it's all about. Now I always wanted to do programming but could never find the right outlet to pursue it further. I'm glad he told me about it cause I went to the class and was so into it that I lost track of time; I knew right then that this was the path for me to take. A couple of months later, here we are, learing Java, HTML, and CSS working to graduate by the middle of December.
